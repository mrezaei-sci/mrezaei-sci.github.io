About

I’m a biomedical engineering researcher working at the intersection of neuroimaging, brain-network modeling, and deep learning. My current work focuses on how practice reshapes cortico–subcortical loops and how network reconfiguration maps onto distinct learning components (accuracy vs. speed/vigor).

I build reproducible pipelines (MATLAB/Python, Linux/Git) and prioritize statistically principled inference (permutation frameworks, family-wise error control).
