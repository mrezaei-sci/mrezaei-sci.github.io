Research Highlights

Motor Skill Learning: Effective‑Connectivity Markers (rs‑fMRI, sDCM)

Resting‑state Spectral DCM Basal ganglia Rehab biomarkers

An end‑to‑end pipeline to quantify directional plasticity in the cortico–basal ganglia–cerebellar loop after short motor practice.

  • What I built: preprocessing → ROI model spec → sDCM inversion → group PEB/BMR/BMA → network visualization.
  • Outputs: subject‑level and group‑level effective‑connectivity change indices for targeted rehabilitation and stimulation planning.
  • Why it matters: provides directional markers of learning‑related reorganization that standard correlation‑based methods miss.
  • Status: toolkit + figures finalized; scaling to larger cohorts.

Creative Playmaking in Soccer: Naturalistic Task‑fMRI with tHOFC

Task‑fMRI tHOFC Sports cognition Decision‑making

A naturalistic freeze‑frame paradigm to capture in‑the‑moment idea generation and its high‑order network signature.

  • What I built: realistic video‑based task; high‑order (correlation‑of‑correlations) connectivity mapping; robust visualization of network reconfiguration.
  • Outputs: reproducible creative‑mode signature linking visual and attention systems without relying on pairwise metrics.
  • Why it matters: informs training protocols and potential neuromodulation to boost on‑field creativity.
  • Status: stimuli + analysis notebooks packaged for reuse.
