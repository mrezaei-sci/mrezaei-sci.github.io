Featured work

Curated case studies that best represent my current research profile.

Rehabilitation AI · Computer vision

Interpretable skeleton-based deep learning for squat-quality assessment

Built an end-to-end movement-assessment pipeline using KIMORE 3D skeleton time series, temporal alignment, body-centered normalization, bone-direction features, motion derivatives, CNN-BiLSTM/TCN models, and Grad-CAM-style explanations for rehabilitation feedback.

KIMORE RGB-D skeletons Movement assessment Explainable AI

NeuroImage · Task-fMRI

Neural efficiency in creative decision-making

Studied creative soccer decision-making using task-fMRI and directed network analysis, identifying a sparse decision-related subnetwork associated with neural efficiency in expert players.

Task-fMRI Soccer experts Directed networks

NeuroImage: Reports · MRI + deep learning

Early Alzheimer's diagnosis with VBM and deep learning

Integrated structural MRI biomarkers with voxel-based morphometry and deep learning, converting biologically meaningful measures into multi-bit heatmap representations for interpretable disease classification.

VBM ADNI CNN/FCN
